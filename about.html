About Markerly.pk

Markerly.pk is a Pakistan-first marketplace designed and owned by Students of IBA to support local creators.

Our vision

Our vision is to be a leading global platform for creators to showcase, sell, and grow through their digital products.

Our mission

OUR MISSION IS TO facilitate emerging creators and businesses by providing an affordable and beginner friendly platform for digital products. We deal with Pakistani market with a main focus to incorporate the use of AI tools to keep up with market trends. We want to support every creator’s passion as everyone deserves a start while being transparent with them. We see ourselves a consumer-focused platform that simplifies the money earning process. We aim to remain profitable by charging fair commission percentage and operating at low-cost. We serve the community through ethical and sustainable ways. We are determined to build a positive image by supporting local talent.

Who we are

Markerly.pk was created as a student project that behaves like a real marketplace, competing with platforms such as Rozee.pk, Fiverr, Upwork and WorkChest, but focused on Pakistani creators and digital products.

Our goal is to give students and early-stage freelancers a safe place to start, earn, and grow while keeping the platform simple, transparent, and aligned with local needs.
