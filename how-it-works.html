How Markerly.pk works

A simple flow for businesses and freelancers to work together on digital products.

For businesses

  1. Describe your project and budget in simple language.
  2. Browse student creators, check profiles, and shortlist 2–3 options.
  3. Agree on scope, place the order, and pay securely.
  4. Review work, request small changes, and approve delivery.
  5. Pay a simple 5% service fee on each completed order.

For freelancers

  1. Create a clear profile with skills, services, and starting price.
  2. Receive project requests from clients in your category.
  3. Confirm requirements and delivery time before starting work.
  4. Deliver files on time and respond to feedback professionally.
  5. Keep 95% of your earnings; only 5% goes to Markerly.pk.
