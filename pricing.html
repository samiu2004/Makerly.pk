Simple 5% + 5% pricing

Markerly.pk keeps pricing easy: 5% from freelancers and 5% from businesses, with no hidden charges.

For freelancers

You keep 95% of every order. Commission is only 5% on each completed project, so more of the money stays with you.

For businesses

A simple 5% service fee gives you access to verified student talent, secure payments, and support.
